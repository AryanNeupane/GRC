# ISMS Implementation Roadmap : CloudNova Technologies

| Phase | Timeframe | Key Activities | Milestone |
|---|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 | Months 1–2 | Context analysis, interested parties, ISMS scope, information security policy, security objectives | ISMS scope and policy approved |
| Phase 2 | Months 3–4 | Risk assessment, risk treatment, control selection, Statement of Applicability (SoA) | Risk treatment plan and SoA approved |
| Phase 3 | Months 5–7 | Control implementation, supporting procedures, security awareness training, evidence collection | Controls operational |
| Phase 4 | Months 8–9 | Performance monitoring, internal audit, corrective actions, management review | Internal audit and management review complete |
| Phase 5 | Months 10–11 | Remediate findings, verify corrective actions, certification readiness review | ISMS ready for certification audit |
| Phase 6 | Month 12 | External certification audit and response to audit findings | Certification achieved, subject to successful audit |
